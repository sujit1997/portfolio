About Me

I am an experienced Automation Developer with over 3 years of expertise in software testing, specializing in both manual and automated testing methodologies. My professional journey includes significant roles at Cognizant Technology Solutions and Krushi Store Enterprises, where I honed my skills in ensuring software quality and delivering efficient, scalable solutions.

Proficient in Java, Selenium, and Maven, I have successfully automated complex functionalities, including the end-to-end testing in domain like Banking & Finance , E-commerce. My background also includes hands-on experience with tools like TestNG, GIT, JIRA, and POSTMAN, and I have a keen interest in Quality Assurance, Performance & Security testing.

With a strong foundation in logical thinking and problem-solving, I am continuously enhancing my skills, including advancing my programming expertise and exploring innovative testing strategies. I aim to contribute to cutting-edge projects by delivering robust, high-quality applications and driving seamless user experiences.
